Scrambled Eggs

Classic & Delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

    • Instructions

    1. First, crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until the yolks and whites are well combined.

    2. Season the eggs with salt and pepper.

    3.Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

    4. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet and let them cook undisturbed for a few seconds until the edges start to set.

    5.Gently scramble the eggs with a spatula, stirring occasionally, until they are cooked to your desired consistency.

    6.Finally, remove from heat and serve hot.
